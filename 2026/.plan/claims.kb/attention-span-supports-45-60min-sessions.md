Most 10-year-olds sustain focused, novel technical work for roughly
45-60 minutes before returns drop sharply, especially when a parent is the
instructor (peer-camp settings can run longer via social energy that a
1:1 home session doesn't have). Sessions should be timeboxed to this and
end on a working state, not "whenever we finish the plan."
