Given the choice of which single piece of "Minecraft" to build small, Abby
picked crafting: gathering resources and turning them into items via
recipes. This is a strong pick for the summer's actual goal (text-syntax
transition) independent of it being her own choice — inventory (a
list/dict), recipes (conditional matching over that list/dict), and
resource-collection events map directly onto real data-structure and
control-flow concepts, more so than a movement/platformer demo would.
Genre and character are still open — this answers "what mechanic," not
the full session-zero agenda.
