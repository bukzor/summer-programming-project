Session length is bounded above by attention span, frequency is bounded
below by memory retention of syntax-level debugging state, and the overall
arc needs an early visible win to convert session-zero enthusiasm into
follow-through once the novelty wears off. Two to four weeks (user,
2026-07-11 — was six to eight, revised once daily/2-3-day cadence made
the old number arithmetically inconsistent with the actual ~9-session
core arc) isn't a pedagogical claim on its own — it's the container the
other two premises get scheduled into, with room to extend past it if she
wants more once the core loop closes
(`../curriculum.kb/design/070-future-work.kb/`).
