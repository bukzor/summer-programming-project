A session that must fit 45–60 minutes for a 10-year-old, and that already
carries the arc's heaviest conceptual load, is the predictable place for an
overrun. Better to pre-plan the split (2a: infrastructure, 2b: pure play)
than to discover it mid-session and end on an unplanned stopping point.
