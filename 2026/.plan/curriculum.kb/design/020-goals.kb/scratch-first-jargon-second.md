When a concept has a Scratch equivalent, introduce it as "the Python spelling
of ⟨block she knows⟩" (see `../../background.kb/scratch-bridge.kb/`). When it
doesn't (dicts, `return`), say so explicitly — "Scratch doesn't have this
one" is itself motivating. When Scratch will *mislead* her, name the
difference before she trips on it.
