Scratch's block model already teaches the hard conceptual content of an
intro course — sequencing, loops, conditionals, variables, events,
broadcast/receive as a message-passing model — without requiring her to
have written a line of text syntax. A kid who finished a Scratch game
project is not starting from zero; she's starting from "knows the ideas,
hasn't met the punctuation."
