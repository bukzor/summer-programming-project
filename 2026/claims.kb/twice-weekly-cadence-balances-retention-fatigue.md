Twice a week is the sweet spot for a summer-only, no-school-pressure
project: frequent enough that she doesn't forget where things left off
between sessions (a real risk with syntax-based debugging, unlike
Scratch's blocks which are self-documenting), infrequent enough that it
doesn't compete with "it's summer, I want unstructured time too."
