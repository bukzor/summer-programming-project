Abby's dedicated hardware is a Lenovo Idea Tab Plus / Tab K12 (ZAG70921US)
— shipped on Android 15, updated to Android 16 as of 2026-07-07 (see
`../abbys-device-log.kb/2026-07-07-os-updated-to-android-16.md`) —
(Dimensity 6400, 8GB RAM, 12.1" touchscreen), not a laptop, with a stand,
external keyboard, and trackpad already available. Plenty of horsepower
for Python + pygame; the open question is software compatibility (Android
vs. desktop Python tooling), not performance.
