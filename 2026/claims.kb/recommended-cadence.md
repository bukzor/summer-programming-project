Two 45-60 minute sessions per week, for 6-8 weeks, each session ending in a
working (not necessarily finished) state. Roughly: weeks 1-2 get a sprite
moving and responding to input; weeks 3-5 add the game's actual mechanic
(whatever she names in session zero); weeks 6-8 add scoring/win-lose state
and polish. Milestones are a scaffold to re-plan against, not a contract —
expect to slip and replan live with her rather than defend the schedule.
