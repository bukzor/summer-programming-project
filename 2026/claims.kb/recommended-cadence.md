Sessions stay at 45-60 minutes. Aim for daily; every 2-3 days is the
realistic floor given actual scheduling
(`../claims.kb/frequent-cadence-balances-retention-fatigue.md`). Initial
horizon: 2-4 weeks to close the core arc, not 6-8 — extend past that only
if she wants more once it's done
(`../curriculum.kb/design/070-future-work.kb/`).

No fixed week-by-week milestone breakdown here (the old "sprite moves by
week 2, scoring by week 6" text described a different tool's arc and was
never accurate for this one — struck 2026-07-11, see
`../curriculum.kb/design/060-deliverables.kb/` for the real session-by-session
plan). What each session actually delivers is named but not pinned this
far in advance, per
`../curriculum.kb/design/020-goals.kb/name-the-choice-dont-pin-it.md` —
resolved session to session, not scheduled as a contract.
