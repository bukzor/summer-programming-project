The curriculum has a strong ending ritual (runnable, commit, log) but no
re-entry step. If retention across the gap between sessions isn't reliable,
an un-scaffolded re-entry risks losing the first several minutes to
reorientation instead of building — a fixed two-minute ritual pays that cost
predictably instead of unpredictably.
