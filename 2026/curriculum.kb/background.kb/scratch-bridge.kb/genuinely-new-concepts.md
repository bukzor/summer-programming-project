Concepts with no Scratch equivalent — say so explicitly; "Scratch doesn't
have this one" is itself motivating (see
`../../design/020-goals.kb/scratch-first-jargon-second.md`).

- **Dicts.** Load-bearing for crafting. Don't lead with Scratch; lead with
  Minecraft — the inventory screen IS a dict drawn on screen, names with
  counts next to them. Also fine: "a whole drawer of Scratch variables, one
  per block, with name-tag labels."
- **`return` values.** Scratch custom blocks do things but never *answer*.
  Bridge via the pointy-Boolean-block framing in `direct-translations.md`.
- **List of lists.** Scratch lists are flat. Frame as "a list of rows."
