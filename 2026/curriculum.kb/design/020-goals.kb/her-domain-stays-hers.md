Any choice that's indifferent to pedagogy — genre flavor, character, world
theme, block names, colors, whether there's a win condition — is 100%
Abby's domain. We do not pre-decide these, even provisionally, even as a
"just a placeholder" best guess. The burden of proof is on treating a
choice as ours; default to hers.

Choices that are technically or pedagogically load-bearing — the engine,
the data structures, the mechanic sequencing that makes next session
possible — are ours to hold fixed as scaffolding she operates inside of,
not something she's asked to relitigate. State which is which out loud
when it isn't obvious (see `name-the-choice-dont-pin-it.md` for how the
plan holds a hers-domain choice open without pinning it).
