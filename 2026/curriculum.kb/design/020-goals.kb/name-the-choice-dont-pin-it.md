Where the plan depends on a choice that's hers to make
(`her-domain-stays-hers.md`), name and define the choice-point far enough
ahead that later sessions can rely on it existing — but don't pin down
what it resolves to until the session that actually needs an answer.
A named-but-open choice in the plan is a placeholder for a live
conversation with her, not a draft answer we're quietly defending.

Resolve it just-in-time: at the point in a session where the choice
actually starts mattering, ask her, then write down what she picked.
Don't resolve it early on her behalf to save planning effort, and don't
leave it so late that a session stalls waiting on a decision that should
have been named already.
