Inventory, recipes, and tool gating are all *data*, not code: a pre-seeded
counter dict, a shapeless recipe dict, and a block→required-tool dict. This
is the load-bearing design choice for session 6 ("the game becomes a game"
with no new code, only new data) — see
`../060-deliverables.kb/session-06-tools-have-rules.md`.
