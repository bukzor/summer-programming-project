Eight fixed sessions (0–7) carry the core arc in strict order — each
introduces exactly what the next needs and no more. Past session 7, the
model inverts: an open expansion menu (`../070-future-work.kb/`) that Abby
picks from, plus a pull-based `ideas.md` backlog for anything she asks for
that isn't listed. Pacing rule: a session finishing early does the stretch
or pulls from `ideas.md` — never starts the next numbered session early.
Ending on a win beats ending on a half-built feature.

Intended cadence: every 2-3 days (`../../../claims.kb/recommended-cadence.md`).
Frequent enough that the opening ritual
(`../060-deliverables.kb/session-facilitation-protocol.md`) suffices to
reorient; loose enough to survive real scheduling. If a gap runs long,
extend the ritual rather than compressing the next session. The code
repo's `sessions.log` provides the baseline once session 0 populates it.
