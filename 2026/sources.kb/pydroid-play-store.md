Official Play Store listing plus surrounding coverage confirming pygame 2
is a free, built-in feature of Pydroid 3 (not premium-gated), with
auto-detection of pygame/sdl2/tkinter/kivy scripts to run them in the
right mode. Premium (~one-time purchase) removes ads and adds
code-prediction/extended keyboard/more libraries (TensorFlow, PyTorch) —
none of which are required to run pygame.
