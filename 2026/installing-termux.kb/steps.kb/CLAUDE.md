# steps.kb

One ordered step of the install-and-verify procedure per file, numbered in
execution order. Each step ends with an explicit **Success** line; known
failures route into `../troubleshooting.kb/`.

Belongs here: anything you must *do* to get from a fresh tablet to verified
pygame-under-Termux -- including the recording and graph-update duties at
the end.

Does not belong: symptom-keyed fixes (`../troubleshooting.kb/`), attempt
history (`../runs.kb/`), rationale for why Termux at all (that debate lives
in `../../deductions.kb/` and `../../claims.kb/`).

When a run deviates from a step as written, patch the step before ending
the session.
