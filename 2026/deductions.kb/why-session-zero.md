Buy-in comes from her making the choices that matter to her (genre,
character, tool) rather than inheriting the parent's choices
(session-zero-improves-buy-in); the early-win premise then forces the
agenda to end with a concrete, small, named target rather than a vague
"we'll figure it out as we go" — otherwise session zero produces
enthusiasm with no way to tell, three sessions in, whether the project is
on track.
