Session length is bounded above by attention span, frequency is bounded
below by memory retention of syntax-level debugging state, and the overall
arc needs an early visible win to convert session-zero enthusiasm into
follow-through in week 3+ when the novelty wears off. Six to eight weeks is
just "a typical summer minus vacation weeks," not a pedagogical claim on
its own — it's the container the other two premises get scheduled into.
