Trinket.io runs Python in-browser (no install) via Skulpt, a JavaScript
reimplementation of Python — not real CPython/pygame. Includes
purpose-built, kid-targeted "Game Development with Pygame Zero" courses.
Full GUI/pygame access appears to require a paid plan per trinket.io/plans
— verify current pricing before committing.
